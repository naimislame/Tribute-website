A Letter to Ibrahim

Dear Ibrahim,

I don’t know how to put everything into words, but I want you to know this—this website is my gift to you. A small space in this big world where your presence still lives on.

Every laugh, every late-night talk, every shared moment still echoes in my heart. Sometimes I still scroll through old messages and smile, sometimes I miss you so deeply it hurts. But you’re still with me—in every memory, in every silent prayer, in every little thing that reminds me of you.

You were not just a friend—you were family. A soul so pure, so vibrant, and so full of love. I hope this small tribute makes you smile from wherever you are. It holds our stories, our memories, and a piece of my heart that will always belong to you.

I know you're always with me. Even if I can't see you, I feel your presence in the quiet moments, in the memories we made, and in the silence that follows laughter.

You are missed. You are remembered. You are loved—always.

With all my heart,
Naim

Forever in our hearts, Ibrahim
